Maptime Summit!

June 8, 2015 // New York, NY // UN Headquarters

Calling all Maptime organizers, attendees, and enthusiasts! Maptime Summit is coming, and we want you to be there.

On Monday, June 8th at the United Nations Headquarters (immediately following State of the Map US 2015), join us for a day full of learning, collaboration, and excitement as we get to know each other and strengthen the ties throughout our international Maptime landscape. During the Maptime Summit, you can expect to:

  • Share your experiences and things you've made at Maptime
  • Organize with other Maptimers around topics you're interested in (like Maptime for Kids!)
  • Get insight on making your own tutorials and better using the ones available
  • Learn how you can get involved with Maptime HQ
  • Strengthen your chapter and your relationships with other chapters
  • Have a TON of fun!

Registration is now open for this historic event. RSVP today and come join us in New York!

Scholarships

We are hoping to provide a few scholarships for those traveling from out of town or who need financial assistance with accommodations. If you are interested in a scholarship, send us an email at hello@maptime.io and we will provide more details.

Volunteers

We will have a variety of volunteer positions available both leading up to and during the event. If you're interested in volunteering, send us an email at [hello@maptime.io](mailto:hello@maptime.io) and we'll be sure to let you know when volunteer signups are available.

Sponsorship

We are looking for sponsors to help us get Maptimers to NYC! If you're interested in funding our scholarships, send us an email at [hello@maptime.io](mailto:hello@maptime.io) and we'll find a time to chat.

See you in New York!
